Frequently Asked Questions

How do you add points?

Once you are signed into your account, you should see a "+" icon on the bottom right. Click on it and fill it out, then your club/sport advisor will grant you points. If they feel like the amount you requested dosent add up, then they will deny your points.

How do you join a club/sport?

Once you are logged in, you have to fill out a form with the clubs and sports that you are in. The advisors of those will check if you are in them and they will grant you permission to join. They can also deny your request if they want to.

Will my points stack up over the years?

Yes, you can stack your points from Freshman year all the way up to the End of Senior Year. Once you graduate, your account will be discontinued. So make sure to spend all your points while you can.
